About System Demos

System Demos provide interactive visualizations of real security tools used in production environments. These demonstrations help you understand how security systems operate and what security analysts see in their daily work.

Important: These demos are for educational visualization only. They do not include exercises, scoring, or feedback. For hands-on practice with evaluation, please visit the Training Center.

How to Use:

  1. 1. Click "Start Capture" or "Start Monitoring" to begin the simulation
  2. 2. Observe events and traffic appearing in real-time
  3. 3. Use filters and search to explore different types of data
  4. 4. Click on individual events/packets for detailed information
