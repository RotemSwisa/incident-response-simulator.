About This Platform

The Incident Response Simulator is a comprehensive training platform designed to help cybersecurity professionals and students develop critical skills in identifying and responding to cyber threats.

Through realistic simulations and detailed learning materials, you'll gain hands-on experience in threat detection, incident analysis, and security response strategies.
